These files store a list of articles and their hyperlink types
Qid = Wikidata id of an item
WP-id = Wikipedia id of an article/a page
For example:
Article "Tokyo" has Wikidata-based type: Capital City (Q1490)
Japan, Tokyo Bay are hyperlinks to Tokyo.
Japan and Tokyo Bay have types: Country (Q6256) and Bay (Q39594)
Country and Bay are hyperlink types of Article "Tokyo"
